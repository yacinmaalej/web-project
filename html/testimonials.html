Testimonials

Omar Frikha

IIT is one of the most prestigious institutions in the country, offering unparalleled education and opportunities in engineering and technology. The faculty are highly experienced, and the campus infrastructure is world-class, with advanced labs and research facilities. IIT has provided me with excellent exposure to both academics and extracurriculars, shaping my career and personal growth immensely.

Asma Karray

While IIT is undoubtedly a top-tier institution, the intense academic pressure can be overwhelming for some students. The curriculum is maintaining a work-life balance. Although the infrastructure is excellent, some hostels and common areas could benefit from modernization. Overall, IIT delivers quality education but could work on improving student mental health support and relaxation opportunities.

Youssef Walha

IIT offers a mixed experience depending on the individual. Academically, it’s top-notch with a reputation that opens doors globally. The peer group is incredibly talented, fostering healthy competition but also adding to stress levels. The campus life is decent, with plenty of cultural and technical events to keep students engaged. It’s a great place if you're ready to put in the effort and handle the pressure.
